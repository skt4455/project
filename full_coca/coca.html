LIFTED AND SHIFTED BRANDS

We scaled glaceau smartwater®, ZICO® coconut water and Appletiser® into new markets. The expansion of our Venturing & Emerging Brands (VEB) model to Europe has shown early signs of success. control_point

ACCELERATED COCA-COLA ZERO SUGAR GROWTH GLOBALLY

We introduced a new formula for Coca-Cola Zero Sugar™ in 20 markets, including the United States, helping deliver double-digit revenue growth for the brand globally. control_point

In Latin America, along with our bottling partners, we acquired AdeS®, the leading soy-based beverage brand in the region. The brand is now being rolled out across key markets in Europe. control_point
